# 7.1 Performance Benchmarking

## Objective
To quantify the system's effectiveness using metrics that reflect real-world agentic utility.

## Benchmarking Dimensions

### 1. Behavioral Accuracy
- **Pass@1**: Percentage of tasks completed successfully on the first attempt.
- **Edit Distance**: For data extraction (OCR), the delta between predicted and ground-truth text.

### 2. Operational Efficiency
- **Tokens-per-Goal**: Total tokens consumed to achieve the objective.
- **Compute-to-Value**: VRAM utilization relative to the complexity of the task.

### 3. Resilience
- **Recovery Rate**: Percentage of tool failures successfully corrected by the Jidoka loop.

## The "Agent-as-a-Judge" Protocol
Use a superior model (e.g., Claude 4 or GPT-5) to audit the execution logs of the local swarm.
- **Judge Instruction**: "Analyze the reasoning trace of this agent. Rate its tool selection efficiency from 1-10 and identify any logical hallucinations."
